OUR PROGRAMM

Connective Tissue Massage

A unique type of bodywork that can be intense and can be painful, however if you are in pain or suffering from an injury such as carpal tunnel, post shoulder, hip or knee surgery, this is the modality you need.

Alchemical Alignment

The emphasis is on creating conditions that orient you to the present time and assist your system to stabilize and then attune my system to yours. The intention is to regulate your system and resolve issues that are holding you back or draining you. This work is for anyone who has experienced trauma of any kind including those struggling with PTSD.

Biodynamic CranioSacral Therapy

A light  touch approach that can create dynamic improvements in your life. It releases tensions deep in your body to relieve pain and dysfunction and improve whole - body health and performance.
